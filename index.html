Simple Man

I'm a simple man. I see skills upgradable by Aghanim's Scepter, I take skills upgradable by Aghanim's Scepter.

Simple Man by Deniz Arsan.

A tool that helps picking abilities in Ability Draft mode in Dota 2.
After selecting the available heroes in the pool, the tool displays abilities that are upgradable by Aghanim's Scepter.

Made using Vue.js and Bulma.
